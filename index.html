Our Story

Discover the Essence of Simo Shop

Founded in 2015 by Chef Simo Benkirane, Simo Shop has quickly become a landmark of culinary excellence in Marrakech. Our philosophy is simple: to create unforgettable dining experiences by combining the finest local ingredients with innovative techniques.

Located in the heart of the Medina, our space blends traditional Moroccan architecture with contemporary design, offering a warm and sophisticated ambiance that reflects our culinary approach.

Every dish tells a story, every ingredient has a purpose, and every visit is an opportunity to embark on a sensory journey through Moroccan flavors with a modern twist.

Simo Benkirane

Founder & Executive Chef
